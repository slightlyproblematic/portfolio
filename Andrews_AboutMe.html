Thomas Andrews

Hi, my name is Thomas Andrews. I've been working for a few years now to accomplish my goal of being an electrical engineer. To accomplish this, I plan on taking my fair share of engineering classes whenever I can. I have been around computers all of my life, but only within the last 5 or so years have I been interested in actually learning about them. I have found myself fascinated by them, and am in the process of learning as much as I can.

I am no stranger to the workplace, and have been employed before. I used to work at Anka BHI, during the summer of 2017, as a file clerk. My job throughout the summer was to scan documents I was given and put them into a digital database. I ran into my share of glitches during that summer- from the database itself breaking, to the hardware malfunctioning. From my past work exploits, I have learned the importance of being able to get into a routine, and being able to break free of it when I need to think critically. I am able to put my nose to the grindstone when needed and just get things done, but am also able to contribute to the ideas I am working on.
